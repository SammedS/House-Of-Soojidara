Our Story

Where Craft Meets Couture

House of Sooji Dhaara is a bespoke boutique born from a love for India's rich textile heritage. Founded by Aishwarya Shyam Sundar, every stitch is a promise — to honour tradition while making it uniquely yours.

We work exclusively on custom orders, which means every garment is made to fit you — your measurements, your vision, your celebration.
